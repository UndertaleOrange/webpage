Who We Are

We are a group of game developers called Omniqube and Sinetic Studio.

We started to actively develop Undertale Orange in March 2017. First two months there was only me, after that Marek Fekete and Matej Misál joined me and we started to grow. We have achieved 10 000+ views on our gamejolt site. In 2018 we called our group Omniqube Software and created a logo for it. Our game won the best story game award in Ihra, Košice. We are currently attending another Ihra in 2019.
